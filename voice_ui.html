Voice UI Sales App

Objective

To design a voice user interface app for Android, for a take-home design task. The brief asked that users should be able to talk into the app and and ask it questions, which it could then answer.

Approach

- Sketched the concepts for the landing page, created the first iteration hi-fi wireframe and later worked on the subscribe / unsubscribe flow and poll interaction design.

- Created a pattern library that would best display information for the different elements of the newsletter, such as callouts for user responses, various polls, special issue boxes on particular topics, and a “at a glance” view on breakthroughs.

Challenges

- Email analytics are basic, meaning it’s hard to quantitatively know how well the template is performing, as the content gets more feedback compared to the design.
- Interactivity in emails is extremely limited. When we used a star feedback mechanism, we were unable to get the other star elements to fill in when a user chose a rating higher than 1 star.
- Convincing the legal team to make the General Data Protection Regulation mandatory text more user friendly and readable.

Design Challenges

- Introduced the polls as a way to gather feedback on the design of the newsletter, not just as a way to find out pertinent information about the audience of the newsletter.
- The final iteration of the landing page used jump links to take users to the input field at the bottom, taking them through compelling reasons they should subscribe. However, the marketing campaigns we ran explained the product to potential subscribers, and when they decided to sign up, our design didn’t allow them to sign up straightaway. We then added an input field above the fold, to solve this issue.

Results

- The landing page had a conversion rate of 83% on desktop in the first few months
- We received glowing feedback about the newsletter from users all over the world
- We managed to grow the subscription list to more than 80k subscribers, from simple marketing, and then managed to hit the 100k target by offering a weekly edition of our newsletter to users subscribed to another marketing email.
